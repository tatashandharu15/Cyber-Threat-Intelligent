# Vision

Build a unified Cyber Threat Intelligence and Digital Risk Protection platform that helps security teams discover, prioritize, investigate, and remediate external threats before they become incidents.

## Product Thesis

Organizations struggle to monitor domains, brands, leaked credentials, phishing infrastructure, dark web exposure, and external asset risk from one workflow. The platform consolidates these signals into one analyst workspace with strong traceability, evidence handling, and action-oriented alerting.

## Outcome

- Reduce time to detect external exposure across brand, asset, and identity surfaces
- Reduce analyst swivel-chair work across disconnected monitoring tools
- Improve executive confidence through auditable reporting and investigation workflows
- Support phased delivery from MVP to enterprise-scale CTI and DRP operations
