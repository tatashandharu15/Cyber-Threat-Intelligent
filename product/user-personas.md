# User Personas

## Security Operations Analyst

- Needs a unified queue of alerts, findings, and evidence
- Prioritizes severity, confidence, asset criticality, and remediation guidance

## Threat Intelligence Analyst

- Needs dark web, breach, phishing, and brand intelligence in one workflow
- Correlates external indicators, actors, campaigns, and recurrence patterns

## Security Administrator

- Manages users, roles, permissions, source access, and platform settings
- Requires strong audit trails, policy enforcement, and secure defaults

## Executive Stakeholder

- Needs trend reporting, risk posture summaries, and measurable outcomes
