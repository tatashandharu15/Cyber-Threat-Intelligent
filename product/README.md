# Product Management

This folder stores strategy, discovery evidence, personas, roadmap, feedback, and measurable outcomes.

## Discovery Connection

- `discovery/` captures customer interviews and problem-space evidence
- `research/` captures market and workflow analysis
- `competitors/` captures competitor positioning and differentiation
- `feedback/` captures ongoing signals from users and operators

## Traceability

- Discovery informs `vision.md`
- Vision shapes `roadmap.md`
- Roadmap and evidence inform PRDs
- PRDs and validated product direction inform SRS and specs
